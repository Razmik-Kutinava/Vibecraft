Common MVP commissioning mistakes — and how to avoid them without slowing down.

## 1. Shipping too much in v1

An MVP should validate **one** hypothesis, not everything at once.

## 2. No success criteria

Without a “what good looks like” metric, you can’t decide what to do next.

## 3. Fuzzy requirements

Clear user stories ship faster than vague feature lists.

## Takeaway

Tighten scope, define metrics, formalize user scenarios.
