Both are great for MVPs, but the trade-offs differ around **pricing**, **vendor lock-in**, and **shipping speed**.

## When Supabase wins

- You want Postgres and a SQL-first model
- You care about predictable early-stage costs

## When Firebase wins

- You want the fastest path with minimal backend code
- A document model fits your domain

## Takeaway

Pick based on your team, data model, and 6–12 month scaling plan.
