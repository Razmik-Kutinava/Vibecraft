I was building a system for a coffee chain **without** living in the repo full-time.

## The rule

- **Three pomodoros max per day** (25 minutes of focus each).
- Roughly **~1.5 hours** of product work total — morning, noon, or evening.

That’s **Pomodoro** plus **Pareto**: every day, do the **20%** that actually moves the needle.

## In practice

- **One screen.**
- **One slice of logic.**
- **One scenario** — not half the product at once.

Over a week it compounds — without the feeling that you must “finish everything today” or snap.

## Why it matters for clients

When a team honestly limits **WIP** and ships **small increments**, the **pace** lasts longer and **weekly demos** are easier: there’s always something to show.

It’s not about working less — it’s about a **sustainable** rhythm.
