Many venues still ship menus as **feed posts** or PDFs. It **works**, but weakly **engages**.

## What Mini Apps add

- Users stay **inside Telegram** — no separate app install.
- You can run a **flow**: quiz → taste → **personal pick** → **promo code** in one chain.
- For seasonal launches it’s a **measurable** funnel from quiz to order.

## When it’s not enough

If you need **deep kitchen** and hardware integrations, a Mini App won’t replace a full **POS** — but it’s a strong **marketing** and **first-touch** layer.

For a **fast MVP** and testing “do people actually enter the seasonal menu,” it’s a **cheap** surface to try.
